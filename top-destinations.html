Sailing in Ibiza-Spain

But what is there to see in Ibiza? Incredible coves and paradisiacal beaches, vegetation and nature... This island has it all! Renting a boat in Italy

You can charter a yacht in Greece and explore all of the surroundings and the copious amounts of islands. Whether you want to charter from Athens or from one of the famous islands, hire a motorboat, sailboat or catamaran as a bareboat or with skipper. When renting a sailboat or motorboat, you’ll have the choice to see Greece by water on the Aegean Sea on the eastern part, the Ionian Sea on the western shore, and the Sea of Crete and Mediterranean directly to the south.

Greece, located on the southern tip of the Balkan peninsula, has common borders with Albania on the northwest, Republic of Macedonia and Bulgaria directly to the north, and Turkey on the furthest eastern point. The geographical diversity of where Greece stands is not just by land, but also by sea.

Charter a yacht in Monaco

Croatia is one of the most popular destinations in the nautical world. With it's beautiful climate and incredible coasts, this has been a sailors dream destination for decades. When arriving, you can hire a boat in Croatia at one of the many marinas available. You can choose among sailboats, motorboats, and catamarans in Croatia that allow you to sail with or without skipper.

If you want to rent a boat in Croatia, you have to book fast. In March, 53% of the boats are already booked. In mid-July and August, this number goes up to 90%. November measures the lowest percentage of boats booked. If you want book a motorboat for the day, a request a few weeks before will be feasible, but a yacht charter will need to be booked months in advance.

Boat Rental in Portugal

Renting a boat in Malta will be sure to give you the most memorable experience possible. The island is perfect for sailing and water activities are extremely popular. Once you visit Malta, you will realize why it is continously one of the most sought out destinations to visit.

Famous for its boat parties, Malta has a lot more to offer for everyone interested in the nautical world. Indeed, Malta should be on the bucket list of every sailor, regardless of their sailing skills. The archipelago is a huge sailing playground and during summer months, the crystal clear water and warm temperatures makes it the perfect destination. Gather your friends and family and hire a boat for a day out on the water.

When to charter a boat in the British Virgin Islands

The one great thing about the BVI yacht charters is that sailing season can be all year round depending on your project. Typically the high season will run from December to April with the islands being pretty busy. During this period, you'll find plenty of regatta's and stronger winds. Going into June, the winds will start to let up and everything will be more peaceful. Keep this in mind when you plan as the high season will have the highest number of tourists and the ports will be much busier.

Keeping this in consideration, the pricing will be different throughout the year. If you plan on sailing during the high season, the prices will average about 500€ per day for a catamaran rental BVI or around 400€ a day for a sailboat.
